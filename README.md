# Career OS

A health-first operating system for coordinating my MScFE, AI engineering, research, projects, and public professional growth.

## Purpose

Career OS exists to support a long-term professional identity:

> An AI engineer specialising in financial AI systems who can research ideas deeply and turn them into reliable, usable software.

It treats job experience, MScFE study, personal engineering, research, and public proof of work as connected parts of one system rather than competing commitments.

## Governing principle

> Protect health, honour fixed commitments, learn with questions, build reusable systems, document decisions, publish only what has substance, and continue without guilt after interruptions.

## Repository role

This repository is the version-controlled source of truth for:

- current priorities and operating mode;
- long-term direction;
- cross-project plans;
- important decisions;
- working standards and reviews as they are introduced.

Implementation code belongs in separate project repositories. Evergreen conceptual notes will later live in Obsidian. Fixed deadlines belong in Calendar.

## Public-information boundary

This repository may contain original plans, reflections, derivations, engineering standards, and links to public work.

It must not contain:

- employer or client confidential information;
- credentials, secrets, or private personal data;
- downloaded WQU course materials;
- graded assessment questions or submissions;
- copyrighted notebooks or datasets that cannot be redistributed.
