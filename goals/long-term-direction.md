# Long-Term Direction

## Professional identity

The target identity is:

> An AI engineer specialising in financial AI systems who can research ideas deeply and turn them into reliable, usable software.

The desired long-term impression is not merely "knows AI" or "completed finance courses," but:

> A person who can research as well as build things.

## Three-year direction

Over the next two to three years, the system should support growth toward:

- stronger financial mathematics and econometrics through the MScFE;
- deeper AI engineering capability in software architecture, testing, deployment, reliability, and system design;
- a coherent specialisation in financial AI systems;
- three to five exceptionally polished flagship projects;
- initial paper reproduction and, later, meaningful extension work;
- a public body of work that demonstrates both theoretical depth and practical engineering;
- informed consideration of a research-oriented PhD, potentially in Scandinavia.

## How the major areas connect

### Professional work

The job provides real engineering exposure: system ownership, retrieval systems, APIs, containers, databases, vector search, computer vision, and client-driven iteration. Confidential work details remain private, but the transferable engineering principles should influence personal standards.

### MScFE

The MScFE is the primary financial-domain learning driver. It is not separate from AI engineering; it shapes the specialisation. Course concepts may produce derivations, reusable modules, experiments, or features in a larger financial AI system.

### Personal engineering and research

Personal work fills gaps that the job and degree do not cover publicly or deeply enough. It should emphasise reproducibility, architecture, tests, documentation, and research grounding.

### Public proof of work

GitHub, Medium, LinkedIn, and the portfolio should reflect meaningful outcomes from learning and building. Content is a by-product, never the driver.

## Flagship-project philosophy

Maintain one active flagship project at a time. Prefer a small number of polished projects over many shallow repositories.

Potential directions to evaluate later include:

- algorithmic trading systems;
- portfolio optimisation;
- financial recommendation systems;
- retrieval and reasoning over financial documents;
- multimodal financial AI.

No direction is selected prematurely. The choice should be informed by MScFE exposure, sustained interest, research value, and engineering depth.

## Research direction

The first research milestones are:

1. learn to read papers with a defined research question;
2. reproduce a result or method faithfully;
3. compare results and document discrepancies;
4. identify a defensible extension;
5. evaluate whether the extension contributes meaningful evidence.

## Non-goals

The system is not designed for:

- rapid job switching;
- certificate accumulation;
- social-media growth as a primary objective;
- constant project creation;
- competing with unsustainable study schedules;
- presenting confidential employer work publicly.

## Success condition

Success is sustained health alongside increasing evidence that financial theory, AI research, and reliable engineering are converging into one professional capability.
